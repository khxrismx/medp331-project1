Beabadoobee's Music Career

Early Life

Beatrice Kristi Laus was born in the Philippines and moved to West London with her family when she was three.

The Birth of Coffee

Beabadoobee was given her first guitar by her father when she was 17. She then began to write her own songs, her first being Coffee. The song grew popularity when it was sampled by Powfu.

EPs

Beabadoobee created several EPs before releasing her first album.

Fake It Flowers

Fake It Flowers is Beabadoobee's debut album. Because her song Coffee was going viral, she felt confined to the genres of pop, rock, and 90s alternative grunge.

Beatopia

Beabadoobee released her second album two years later. Beatopia, as the name suggests, represents a perfect imaginary world. This album was inspired by her childhood.

This Is How Tomorrow Moves

This Is How Tomorrow Moves is Beabadoobee's latest album release. It has a blend of jazzy instrumentals and soft melodies.
